Welcome to Abeokuta Ai Developer Fellowship

Welcome to the session! We are delighted to have you here as we begin this exciting program together. This welcome session is designed to help you get oriented, understand what to expect, and set you up for success. During this session, we will:

  • Introduce the facilitators, organizers, and your fellow participants.
  • Outline the goals and objectives of the program, so you know what you will gain by the end.
  • Walk you through the schedule, highlighting key activities, important dates, and milestones.
  • Explain the resources and support systems available, including how to access materials, get help, and connect with others.
  • Discuss the rules, guidelines, and expectations for participation, collaboration, and communication.
  • Answer any questions you may have and address any concerns.

This is also a chance for you to share your background, interests, and what you hope to achieve. We encourage you to engage, ask questions, and make connections with your peers. Our goal is to create a welcoming, inclusive, and productive environment where everyone can learn and grow.

We look forward to a great session and a successful journey together!
